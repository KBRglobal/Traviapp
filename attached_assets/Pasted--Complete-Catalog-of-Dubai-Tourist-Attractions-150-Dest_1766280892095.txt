## Key Practical Information

**Seasonal Attractions**: Dubai Miracle Garden and Butterfly Garden operate November-May only. Global Village runs October-May. Camel racing season is October-April; horse racing November-March.

**Best Visiting Period**: October to April offers the most comfortable weather (24-35°C) for outdoor activities. Summer months (May-September) see temperatures exceeding 40°C, making indoor attractions and water parks essential.

**Combination Passes**: Dubai Pass and Dubai Explorer Pass offer savings on multiple attractions. Dubai Parks and Resorts multi-park tickets cover Motiongate, Legoland, Real Madrid World, and water park.

**Transport Tips**: Dubai Metro's Red Line connects Dubai Mall (Burj Khalifa station) and Mall of the Emirates (Mall of the Emirates station). Water transport via abra (AED 1) and ferry provides scenic alternatives.

This catalog represents Dubai's extraordinary concentration of world-class attractions, confirming its status as a global tourism leader with experiences spanning every category from extreme adventure to cultural immersion.
