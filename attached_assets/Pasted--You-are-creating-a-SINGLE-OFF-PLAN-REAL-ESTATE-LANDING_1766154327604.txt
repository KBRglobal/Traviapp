- Major developers (Emaar, Sobha) rarely make significant changes

**Impact:** Minor changes (fixture brands, layouts) are common and usually don't affect value significantly.

**Risk:** If you miss payment milestones, developer can cancel contract and keep paid amounts per SPA terms.

**Mitigation:**

**Impact:** Contract cancellation, loss of down payment and paid installments. Always ensure you can meet payment obligations.
