	•	❌ נקודתיים פחות אידאליים
	•	❌ אין הרחבת intent

Dubai Mall Visitor Guide 2025 – Attractions, Shopping & Tips | Travi

Explore Dubai Mall with this complete visitor guide to the world’s largest shopping center, including attractions, prices, shopping tips, and insider advice
