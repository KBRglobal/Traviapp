### **A. General Draft Rules**
- Draft-only mode: אין פרסום אוטומטי
- Expandable sections: intro, dining/activities, FAQ
- Images: optimized filenames, alt texts, compressed for web
- Internal linking: optimize for SEO (4–10 links per page)
- CTA: primary CTA above the fold, mid-page, final section
- Trust signals: near all CTA buttons
- Schema: TouristAttraction / Hotel / Article / FAQPage / Breadcrumbs
- Word count targets:
  - Attractions ~1950 words
  - Hotels ~3000 words
  - Articles variable (~1200–2000 words)
- Tone & personality: per content type (A–E)
- Marketing words: max 3–5 per article, supported by facts

### **D. Automated Travel Articles (RSS Feed)**
1. **Analyze RSS Feed**
   - Identify category (Attractions, Hotels, Food, Transport, Events, Tips, News, Shopping)
   - Urgency Level (Urgent / Relevant / Evergreen)
   - Target audience (Families, Couples, Backpackers, Luxury, Business)
   - Unique angle (value, unique experience, convenience)
   - Select Personality & Tone (A–E)

2. **Choose Structure**
   - News + Guide / Structured List / Comparative / Story + Info / Problem-Solution / News Update

3. **SEO & Headlines**
   - Primary/Secondary/LSI keywords
   - URL structure
   - Meta title + description

4. **Content Creation**
   - Opening paragraph (hook, primary keyword)
   - Quick Facts box
   - Main content (sections H2/H3, expandable)
   - Pro Tips / Insider Tips
   - Warnings / Good to Know
   - Internal links
   - Images + ALT text
   - Practical info (prices, hours, directions)
   - Final Thoughts / CTA
   - Maintain human tone, sentence variation, 3–5 marketing words max
